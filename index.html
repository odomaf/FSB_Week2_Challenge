my projects

PRI Pest Degree Day Calculator

Designed to increase crop yields for 120,000+ farmers across Illinois by predicting pest activity to inform strategic farming decisions. Modeled current and future pest activity on more than a decade of historical data in concert with NOAA weather information. (VB.net, asp.net, javascript, python, ARCgis, SOAP, css, html, MS SQL Server)

R2D2: Relocate To/Deliver To

Inventory relocation tool designed to minimize misplacement of University equipment, reduce workload of inventory management team, and streamline the inventory relocation process for five scientific surveys spanning 99 locations.
